It is easy to create a visual record of your running with RunFree, RunMe. Simply upload a photo to Instagram or find a picture on instagram that you like or find inspirational. When you view a picture on Instagram, there is a button to the lower right side of the picture. If you click it, you can get an embed code, which you can insert into this form.
